About us

Bella is a brand under K-Roma Limited located in Kampala, Uganda. The brand was established in 2002 by Prudence Ukkonika and is the most dominant organic wine producer and distributor in Uganda today.

Bella manufactures tea, wine and juice from local tropical fruits and currently has adistribution network throughout East Africa. We strive to safest and healthiest organic products to our customers.

On top of the hundreds of people, we employ, we procure our resources from small local farmers and ensure that they lead a life with improved conditions
